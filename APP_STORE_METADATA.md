## Description (4000 chars max)

Swish Stats is a free, FIBA-compliant basketball statistics recorder built for coaches, team managers, and stat keepers. Record live game stats courtside with a clean, fast interface designed for the pace of real basketball.

LIVE GAME SCORING
• Tap to record 2-pointers, 3-pointers, and free throws for both teams
• Track assists, rebounds, steals, blocks, turnovers, and fouls per player
• Real-time scoreboard updates as you input stats
• Support for quarters or halves format with configurable period length
• Undo last action to correct mistakes instantly

SHOT CHARTS
• Visual half-court diagram showing where each shot was taken
• Made vs missed shots displayed by location
• Identify shooting hot zones and cold spots at a glance

BOX SCORES & GAME REVIEW
• Full NBA-style box score with every stat category
• Team comparison side-by-side (points in paint, second chance points, bench points)
• Per-quarter scoring breakdown
• Play-by-play event log

PLAYER PROFILES
• Season averages: PPG, RPG, APG, FG%, 3P%, FT%, EFF
• Game-by-game performance history with W/L results
• Track development across the entire season

TEAM MANAGEMENT
• Create and manage multiple teams
• Full roster management with player names, numbers, and positions
• Works for any league — FIBA, NBL, school, social, or club basketball

SEASON STATS
• Team win/loss record
• Season averages for all major statistical categories
• Opponent points per game and defensive metrics

PDF REPORTS
• Generate professional game reports with full box scores
• Share reports via email, messaging, or AirDrop
• Perfect for post-game analysis and team meetings

WORKS OFFLINE
• All data stored locally on your device — no internet required during games
• Perfect for gyms and courts with poor WiFi
• Your data stays private on your phone
