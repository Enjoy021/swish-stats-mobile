# Privacy Policy — Swish Stats

**Last Updated:** 25 March 2026  
**Effective Date:** 25 March 2026

Swish N' Dish Sporting Good PTY LTD ("we", "our", "us") operates the Swish Stats mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App stores all data — including teams, players, games, and statistics — locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

### Data Stored on Your Device

- Team names and configurations
- Player names, numbers, and positions
- Game scores, statistics, and play-by-play data
- Shot chart locations
- Season records and averages

This data remains entirely on your device and is never uploaded, shared, or accessible to us.

## 2. Data Collection and Transmission

- **No accounts or sign-ups required** — the App works immediately without registration
- **No analytics or tracking** — we do not use any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or similar technologies
- **No advertising** — the App contains no ads and no ad-related data collection
- **No server communication** — the App functions fully offline with no network requests for data storage
- **No location data** — we do not access or store your location

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party data collection services. The only network communication occurs if you choose to:

- **Share PDF reports** — When you export a game report as a PDF and share it via iOS Share Sheet, the sharing is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We do not intercept or store shared content.
- **Over-the-Air (OTA) Updates** — The App may check for updates via Expo's update service. This connection transmits only technical metadata (app version, runtime version, platform) necessary to determine if an update is available. No personal data is sent.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- **Your data persists** as long as the App is installed
- **Uninstalling the App** permanently deletes all associated data
- **You control your data** — you can delete teams, players, and games at any time within the App

We have no ability to access, recover, or delete your data because we never receive it.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected or transmitted, the App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent Australian regulations.

## 6. Security

Your data is stored using your device's standard application storage (AsyncStorage). The security of this data depends on the security measures of your device (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, etc.). We recommend keeping your device software up to date and using a device passcode.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 8. Australian Privacy Act Compliance

As an Australian company, we comply with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). Given that the App does not collect personal information, the APPs regarding collection, use, disclosure, and security of personal information are not triggered. Should this change in future versions, we will update this policy accordingly and ensure compliance with all applicable Australian privacy laws.
